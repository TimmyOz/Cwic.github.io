Friendly and Affordable House Cleaning


We're a local father-son team that specializes in cleaning houses and rental properties which need a deep clean in-between tenants. Having 20+ years of owning and cleaning properties of our own, we have gained a lot of experience cleaning and fixing rental units after tenants have moved out.

We offer services such as junk removal and general cleaning around the house. Cleaning would include tasks such as scrubbing and/or sweeping floors, walls, windows, mirrors, toilets, etc. Junk removal would include anything deemed as junk, except anything hazardous, inside or outside the property. price would be based on how much junk is removed.

Please contact Coltyn or Craig for a quote or consultation if you are interested in any of our services.
