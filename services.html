Services


We offer quality cleaning services for houses, condos, and apartments as well as a variety of other services to fit your needs and budgets. We only use Eco-friendly cleaners, supplies and equipment. Our business is also insured and bonded for your peace of mind. Recurring cleanings can be scheduled to fit your timeline and schedule!

Deep Cleans

Our deep cleans include:

  • Dusting of all surfaces
  • Vacuum all carpet and rugs
  • Floors swept and mopped(including hardwood)
  • Bathrooms cleaned including toilets, sinks, counters, mirrors, tubs and showers
  • Kitchens cleaned including cooktop, counters, sinks, outside of appliances, microwave inside and out
  • Other services available upon request
